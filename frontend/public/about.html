Who We Are

We are Ainissa & Zhaniya — media technology students exploring the intersection of
cinema and artificial intelligence.

We are students of Media Technology at Astana IT University, united by our passion for visual storytelling and cinema. In our future careers, Ainissa dreams of working as a film editor, shaping rhythm and emotion through montage, while Zhaniya aspires to be a cinematographer, capturing meaning through light and movement.

The rapid growth of artificial intelligence is transforming the way films are made and perceived. Technologies that once belonged to science fiction are now editing, generating, and even performing alongside humans. This evolution raises deep ethical and artistic questions: Can AI truly create emotion? Can we trust what we see on screen?

On social media, millions of clips are created by AI every day. Under each post, one comment repeats again and again — “Is this real or AI?” People begin to doubt their own perception. That question inspired us to create AI Cinema Lab — a space where art, ethics, and technology meet to explore trust and truth in visual storytelling.

Our mission is to study and visualize how AI changes the way we create and perceive films. We aim to build awareness about the new boundaries between authenticity and simulation — and to remind that even in a digital world, emotion is human.

  • Exploring AI’s impact on film professions
  • Reflecting on truth and perception in cinema
  • Combining creativity, ethics, and technology

We envision a future where AI and humans collaborate — where technology becomes not a replacement for creativity, but its expansion.

At AI Cinema Lab, we believe in transparency, empathy, and creative courage. Technology should amplify human imagination — not erase it.
